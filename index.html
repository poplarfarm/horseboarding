About Me

My name is Kira Asher, and I've owned horses for as long as I can remember. I got my first horse when I was four years old and they've been a passion of mine ever since. I've worked with Western disciplined horses for ten years and English for six. I've had training, working, and boarding experience with everything from Clydesdales, to miniature ponies, to Thoroughbreds with foals at their sides.

Mission Statement

I know that having horses can be difficult if you don't have the space or the utilities necessary to keep them healthy. They're a super expensive hobby/lifestyle so I'm here to make sure you can keep your four-legged kids happy while not breaking the bank.
